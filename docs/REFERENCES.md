## Acid-Base Compensation Notes

- UCSF Hospital Handbook. Algorithm for Acid-Base Disorders.
  https://hospitalhandbook.ucsf.edu/01-algorithm-acid-base-disorders/01-algorithm-acid-base-disorders
- Winter's formula and conventional metabolic alkalosis / respiratory compensation rules are used
  here as pragmatic educational Boston-style checks. They do not replace clinical context.
- Corrected anion-gap context is displayed as a teaching aid using conservative thresholds
  selected for this app: elevated above 12 mmol/L and clearly elevated at 16 mmol/L or higher.
  Local laboratory ranges may differ.
